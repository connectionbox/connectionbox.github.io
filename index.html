Connection Box

Micah Fullinfaw - Hannah Gish - Lincoln Graham - Luke Davidson - Peter Schmit

An audiovisual installation combining binaural audio with biofeedback-based generative art, guiding audiences toward a purer connection with themselves and others.

Created in Spring 2024 for the Biodesign Challenge by students at the Johnny Carson Center for Emerging Media Arts.
