Connection Box

An audiovisual installation combining binaural audio with biofeedback-based generative art, guiding audiences toward a purer connection with themselves and others.

Created in Spring 2024 for the Biodesign Challenge by students at the Johnny Carson Center for Emerging Media Arts.

Hannah Gish

Hannah is an aspiring creative technologist and art engineer. She strives to create art that evokes emtional responses and meaning. She is also a lover of iced coffee and collecting cool t-shirts! Find more of her work at hannahgish.com.

Luke Davidson

Tall. Brown hair. Berkenstocks. These are the words that best define Luke Davidson. In addition to helping design the structure, he aided in both the construction of the piece and in the production of electronic components. He also edited the video.

Micah Fullinfaw

A human inspired by human energy. Micah loves experiencing the gifts of different cultures around the world. His art often reflects this desire for an interconnected humanity. He also likes throwing rocks into lakes! Find more of his work at mfullinfaw2.myportfolio.com.

Richard Lincoln Graham IV

Researcher on The Connection Box. Helped in the development, design, and sourcing of materials for the piece. Interested in the intersection of biology and technology.

Peter Schmit

Multimedia artist, multi-instrumentalist and all-around geek. Made the music for this installation, wrangled with the technical details, and made this website. Find more of his work at surrealducks.github.io.
